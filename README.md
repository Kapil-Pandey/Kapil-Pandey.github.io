##  Featured Projects

### 1. Mining Safety Analysis & Prediction
**[View Full Notebook](./projects/Mining_Accident_Analysis_Project.ipynb)**

Comprehensive analysis of 25 years of MSHA accident data (270,000+ incidents) to predict severe accidents and identify critical risk factors in the mining industry.

**Key Achievements:**
- 99.99% ROC-AUC in predicting severe accidents
- Identified 20+ critical-risk mines requiring immediate intervention
- Discovered 12 distinct accident patterns using NLP clustering (SBERT + K-Means)
- Built interactive Gradio dashboard for stakeholders
- Composite risk scoring system combining frequency, severity, and days lost

**Technologies:** Python, Random Forest, Logistic Regression, Linear SVC, SBERT, K-Means, YAKE, Gradio, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

**Business Impact:** 30-40% projected reduction in severe accidents, annual cost savings from reduced insurance premiums and regulatory fines.

### 2. Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB )Consumer Complaints
**[View Full Notebook](./projects/CFPB_Consumer_complaint.ipynb)**

NLP-powered analysis of JPMorgan Chase consumer complaints (2019-2025) to uncover recurring issues, geographic patterns, and product-specific pain points.

**Key Achievements:**
- Topic modeling using TF-IDF + K-Means clustering
- Discovered 4 states (CA, TX, FL, NY) account for 50% of total complaints
- Identified fraud/unauthorized transactions as most frequent complaint
- Built "Complaint Intelligence" dashboard framework

**Technologies:** Python, NLP, TF-IDF, K-Means Clustering, Spacy, Text Mining, Pandas, Visualization

**Business Impact:** Data-driven complaint reduction strategy, improved customer experience, enhanced regulatory compliance.

### 3. Bank Fraud Detection System
**[View Full Notebook](./projects/Fraud_bank_transactions.ipynb)**

Statistical analysis and anomaly detection to identify fraudulent banking transactions, including account takeovers, card skimming, and multi-device fraud patterns.

**Key Achievements:**
- Detected multiple fraud patterns through ANOVA hypothesis testing
- Identified same-device multi-account login breaches
- Mapped geographic fraud hotspots (Fort Worth, Oklahoma City, Charlotte)
- Proposed 2FA authentication and account lockout mechanisms

**Technologies:** Python, ANOVA, Pandas, NumPy, Geospatial Analysis (Folium), Matplotlib, Seaborn

**Business Impact:** Enhanced fraud detection capabilities, reduced account compromise incidents, improved customer security.

---

### 4. Telco Customer Churn Prediction
**[View Full Notebook](./projects/Telco_Customer_Churn.ipynb)**

Predictive modeling to identify at-risk customers for TelcoConnect, enabling proactive retention strategies and reducing customer acquisition costs.

**Key Achievements:**
- Built high-accuracy churn prediction models
- Identified tenure (<1 year) and contract type (month-to-month) as key drivers
- Flagged monthly contract holders as high-risk segment
- Developed actionable retention campaign recommendations

**Technologies:** Python, Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost, KNN, Feature Engineering, Scikit-learn, ROC-AUC Analysis

**Business Impact:** Targeted retention campaigns, reduced churn rate, improved customer lifetime value.
